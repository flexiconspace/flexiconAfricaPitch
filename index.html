3D printing houses being built are made using concrete & water. We do not want to waste the most valuable resource for construction.

Instead we use solar sintered sand and recyclable materials such as plastic in our constructions.

3D printing allows designers, architects and builders to construct any type of home in any shape.

Improve aesthetics of new Africa buildings and areas due to improved architectural capabilities.
