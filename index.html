Nigeria / approx 22 million housing deficit

PRODUCT IDEA

Customized and algorithmic design for each rural area.

Multi-purpose design: originally designed for two families, can be used for a big family or as an office.

Having a sun-finder solar panel.

Rapid construction and economic

PROTOTYPE

Bioclimate design: rotating module to allow passive cooling and natural lighting.

Circular design: 3D printed housing unit with recycled plastics or other locally-available materials.

Having water-detector sensors to activate the innovative flood-proof mechanism.

Possibility of floating if flood water is higher than 2 meters thanks to new anchor foundations.

3D printing

3D printing houses are made using concrete & water. We do not want to waste the most valuable resource for construction.

Instead we use solar sintered sand and recyclable materials such as plastic in our constructions.

3D printing allows designers to construct any type of home in any shape.

Improve aesthetics of new Africa buildings and areas due to improved architectural capabilities.
